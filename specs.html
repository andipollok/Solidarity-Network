Business Rules

User profiles

Only Users can modify their profile's personal details.

Groups ownership and membership

Groups have one or more owners (Users).

The first Group owner is the User who created the Group.

Group ownership can be transferred to another User.

Users can be members of Groups.

Group owners are also obviously members.

Group owners can revoke any User's membership from their Group.

Users' Group membership must be approved by the Group owner.

Activities

All Activities are public (you don't need to be logged in to see them).

An Activity always belong to a Group.

Only members of a Group can publish Activities in it.

Activity publishing in a Group does not require owner's approval.

An Activity can be deleted by the User who created it, and by the owner of the containing Group.

Users can register to any Activity in the Groups they are members of.

Being registered to an Activity means a User will receive notifications* specific to that Activity.

Messaging

Users can send individual direct messages to the other members of the Groups they are members of.

Group owners can send bulk messages (mailing list) to all their members at once.

Stories

Users can post stories in the Groups they are members of.

Story publishing in a Group does not require owner's approval.

A Story can be deleted by the User who created it, and by the owner of the containing Group.

Photos

Users can post photos in the Groups they are members of.

Photo publishing in a Group does not require owner's approval.

A Photo can be deleted by the User who created it, and by the owner of the containing Group.

* Alerts? News?
